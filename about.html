The Easy Distance Unit Converter began as a student group project for a Human Computer Interaction course at Wayne State University in the fall of 2018. The initial product was launched in early December of that same year.

The basic principle guiding the developers of the Easy Distance Unit Converter are simplicity and ease of use. After simply entering three inputs with the keyboard, the user will receive the converted value with stroke of the enter key.

The project is hosted on github.
